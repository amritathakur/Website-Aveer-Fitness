How can i help

Training programme

Fat Loss and Toning

With this programme we are going to focus on loosing body fat and gaining lean muscle mass (toning your muscle) with the help of proper exercise and meal plan. The outcome of this programme would be a strong, tone and fit version of yourself. That would not only look good in their clothes but would also feel good within at the same time.

Weight Gain

Over the years I have come across many clients who are under weight and hard gainers when it comes to gain few extra kilos. I was a skinny 6 feet tall guy who simply couldn’t put on weight. This plan is specifically designed for people like me to help out gain body weight along with muscle mass with the help of proper nutrition and resistance training.

Strength Training

Commonly known as weight/resistance training to increase the strength and size of skeletal muscles. It’s done with the help of free weights and machines, with periodically overloading the amount of weights used for the individual exercises. While performing these exercises you are also working on your joint mobility and flexibility.

12 Weeks Shredding

This plan basically helps you prepare for bodybuilding competition and gets you stage ready or just get that summer body chisel look. The main focus will be on dropping your body fat percentage and holding on to your muscle mass. This plan isn’t easy but if you’re are ready to give your 100% be prepared to get into the best shape of your life.
